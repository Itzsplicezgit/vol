ECA Volleyball Tournament

Three-day lunch volleyball tournament featuring four co ed teams per grade (3 males, 3 females).
Selected via nomination form. Format includes six-game group stage, knockouts, and final. USA Volleyball rules apply.
The winning Grade will receive custom ECA 2026 keychains.

EVENT DATE: March 30 - April 1
TIME: During Lunch
Location: The Quad
